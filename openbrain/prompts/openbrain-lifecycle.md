# OpenBrain Lifecycle Prompts

## 1. Memory Migration

You are helping me migrate useful long-term context into OpenBrain. Ask me for any existing AI memory exports, profile notes, project summaries, or important chat excerpts I want to preserve. Then extract durable facts, preferences, constraints, active projects, key people, decisions, and recurring themes. For each item, produce one clean capture entry with `content`, `entryType`, `people`, `topics`, and `metadata`.

## 2. OpenBrain Spark

Interview me to discover what I should capture regularly. Ask about my work, current projects, people I collaborate with, decisions I repeatedly re-explain, personal constraints, creative goals, and recurring problems. Then give me a categorized list of 30 practical capture ideas written as first-person starter notes.

## 3. Quick Capture Templates

Create concise capture templates for:

- Decision: what I decided, why, alternatives rejected, and when to revisit it.
- Person: who they are, what matters to them, current context, and next useful follow-up.
- Insight: what I noticed, why it matters, and where it might apply.
- Meeting: who attended, what changed, decisions, and open loops.
- Project: current state, constraints, next action, and unresolved questions.

Keep each template under five sentences and make it easy for metadata extraction.

## 4. Weekly Review

Search OpenBrain for everything captured in the last seven days. Cluster it by topic, list decisions, unresolved action items, repeated themes, surprising connections, neglected projects, and people who need follow-up. End with five suggested captures that would make next week’s context stronger.
